Reflection
This is the bouncing off of wave when it hits an obstacle. The wave formed depends on the obstacle they meet.
Diffraction
This is the spreading of wave around corners or around an obstacle. It is more noiceable when the wavelength is longer than the obstacle.
Interference
This is the effect produced when two waves of the same frequency and,amplitude and wavelength travelling in the same direction are superposed as they simultaneously pass through a given point.
